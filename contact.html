Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often. Still have more? Just send us a message above.

How do I book a travel package?

Simply browse our packages, click "Book Now" and fill in your details, or contact us directly via this form or phone and our team will handle everything for you.

Can I customise an existing package?

Absolutely! All our packages are fully customisable. Tell us your preferences — dates, budget, activities — and we'll tailor the perfect itinerary just for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 30 days before departure. Cancellations within 30 days may incur a partial charge depending on the package and supplier terms.

Do your packages include flights?

Most of our packages include return flights. Some are land-only, which is clearly marked. We can also add flights to any land-only package upon request.

Is travel insurance included?

We strongly recommend travel insurance and can arrange comprehensive coverage for you as part of your booking. It covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and more.

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours during working days. For urgent matters, please call our phone line for an immediate response.
